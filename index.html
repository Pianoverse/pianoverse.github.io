About

Pianoverse App is an outcome of more than a year long research for the best Piano playing and learning experience on mobile phones. You can listen, play, learn, and perform your favorite piano covers anywhere, even while traveling.

Compared to other apps, it gives an ad-free experience, new songs unlocks at very low cost, and global leaderboard rankings. You can even connect this app to your digital piano via Midi USB cable and play & learn songs.

Additionally, Pianoverse has Indian key & music notation and plenty of Indian Music content for all Indian music lovers. Few of our main features are the following:

  • Customizable Piano Activity
  • Indian Music Beats, Notations, and Key Labels
  • No Unwanted Advertisements
  • Indian Music and Songs Covers to Learn
  • Regular New Songs Updates
  • No User Data Storage And Sharing
  • Global Leaderboards for Performances
  • Save and Share Song Recordings vis Midi
  • Midi Piano/Device Connectivity via USB cable
  • Transpose Songs to Match Your Favorite Scale
  • Weekly Competitions (Coming Soon)
